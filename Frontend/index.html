Admission Process

  1. Fill out the online admission form
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Attend admission test (if applicable)
  4. Admission result announcement
  5. Complete admission formalities

Required Documents

  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school transfer certificate
  • Mark sheets of previous classes
  • Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
  • Parent/Guardian ID card copy
