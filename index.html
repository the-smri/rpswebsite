About Reaz Public School

The Beginning of the School‚Äôs Journey

At the start of 2023, Reaz Public School began its journey as part of the corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiative of the Epyllion Group. The school was built on 50 percent of a land area in Noadda, Narayanganj, featuring a five-story building with a modern architectural design.The school is not just a structure of brick and concrete; it is a symbol of new hope. It was founded by Reaz Uddin Al Mamun, the Chairman of Epyllion Group.

With an open playground, greenery all around, and classrooms filled with natural light and fresh air, the school creates a free and inspiring learning environment. Here, children are encouraged to explore a world far beyond textbooks a vibrant space for curiosity and growth.

‚ÄúOur goal is not just to build a school, but to transform the entire community. Reaz Public School is one important step toward that change.‚Äù

‚Äî Nazmul Ahsan Sarup, Assistant General Manager, Epyllion Group

Currently, the school has 192 students from preschool to grade seven. Each class has a maximum of 24 students, supported by 10 teachers. An academic mentor oversees the overall learning process. Students who need extra help receive support in separate classes, while the talented learners participate in self-learning groups where they teach and support one another. The school‚Äôs teaching methods are innovative and student-centered.

NASA Astro Camp Partner

Reaz Public School is officially listed as a NASA Astro Camp Community Partners (ACCP) site in Bangladesh. The teachers of this school have received training from NASA to conduct ACCP camps for students.
